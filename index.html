ABOUT ME

I’m Luke—a data and intel analyst, and perpetual problem solver with over two decades of hands-on experience in the data world. My professional journey has taken me from military intelligence and defense contracting to advanced analytics for private-sector clients. I genuinely enjoy staring at data until patterns and trend lines start to emerge, transforming raw information into actionable insight. (Yes, I keep a deep supply of Visine handy for when all that focused staring dries out my eyeballs.)

My work lives at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and real-world decision-making. I’ve tackled everything from SQL databases and Python automation to complex statistical modeling, and I’m always eager to dive into raw, messy data. For me, great analysis isn’t just about clever algorithms—it’s about telling a story that actually leads to action.

When I’m not working, you’ll usually find me reading about the latest in data science, hiking National Parks with my wife and kid, or outside running miles and miles—hoping that in my later years, I’ll still be able to move around decently well.
